UCD Pedestrian Advocacy Group (September 2015)

http://jazhen.github.io/ucd-pedestrian-advocacy-group/index.html

Written using HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap 3, and JavaScript.

Created a website from scratch for UWP 104A - Business Writing with Katie Arosteguy at UC Davis. This was an individual project that was part of a larger group research project that required us to write a formal proposal on a local issue. My group was titled the UC Davis Pedestrian Advocacy Group and we wrote on a safety issue occuring at an intersection in downtown Davis. This website showcases the UC Davis Pedestrian Advocacy Group's work.
